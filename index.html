Emergency Go Bag Essentials
WATER
  • At least 1 gallon (4 liters) per person per day (for 3 days minimum)
  • Water purification tablets or a portable filter (like a LifeStraw)
  • Collapsible water container for storage
FOOD

Non-perishable food (enough for 3 days):

  • Canned meat, fruits, vegetables
  • Energy or protein bars
  • Dried fruits or nuts
  • Instant noodles or oatmeal
FIRST AID SUPPLIES
  • Adhesive bandages (various sizes)
  • Antiseptic wipes or alcohol
  • Pain relievers (e.g., paracetamol, ibuprofen)
  • Prescription medicines (if needed)
  • Burn ointment or antiseptic cream
  • Gauze pads and adhesive tape
PERSONAL & IMPORTANT ITEMS
  • Identification cards and copies of important documents (kept in a waterproof pouch)
  • Cash (small bills and coins)
  • Notebook and pen
  • Personal hygiene items (toothbrush, soap, tissues, feminine products)
